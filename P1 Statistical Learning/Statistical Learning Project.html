4a) Based on the question, we need to perform 2 sample t test¶

here H0: u1=u2 (mean of charges for people who smoke and who don't are equal)¶
=> Charges of people who smoke doesn't differ from charges of people who don't.¶
Ha: u1!=u2 (mean of charges differ for smokers and non smokers)¶
=> Charges of people who smoke differ significantly from charges of people who don't.¶

Let's Assume alpha (level of significance=0.05)¶
